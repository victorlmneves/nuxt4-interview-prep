Tech Lead – Product Engineering
FinanceFlow · Lisbon (Hybrid, 3 days/week) · Full-time

ABOUT THE ROLE
We're looking for a Tech Lead to own the technical direction of our core product — a B2B SaaS platform for financial operations teams. You'll split your time between hands-on engineering (60%) and technical leadership (40%): setting architecture standards, running design reviews, mentoring mid/senior engineers, and collaborating with product and design.

This is not a pure management role. We expect you to ship code every sprint.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Own architecture decisions for the frontend and backend of the platform
- Lead a team of 5 engineers; run 1:1s, unblock, mentor, grow them
- Define and enforce coding standards, review PRs, champion quality
- Collaborate with the CTO on technical roadmap and quarterly planning
- Drive adoption of best practices: observability, testing, CI/CD, incident response
- Work closely with Product and Design from discovery through delivery

REQUIREMENTS — MUST HAVE
- 6+ years of software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in TypeScript, both frontend (Vue or React) and backend (Node.js)
- Experience with PostgreSQL or another relational database at scale
- Solid understanding of cloud infrastructure (AWS or GCP preferred)
- Prior experience leading or formally mentoring engineers
- Comfortable owning incidents and on-call responsibilities
- Fluent English (working language of the team)

REQUIREMENTS — NICE TO HAVE
- Experience with GraphQL APIs
- Familiarity with infrastructure-as-code (Terraform, Pulumi)
- Background in fintech, payments, or regulated industries
- Experience with Nuxt or Next.js

TECH STACK
Vue 3 · Nuxt 3 · TypeScript · Node.js · GraphQL · PostgreSQL · Redis · AWS · Docker · Terraform · GitHub Actions · Datadog

- Hybrid work (Lisbon office, 3 days/week)
